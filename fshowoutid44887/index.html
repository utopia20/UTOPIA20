WALK OF FAME

UTOPIA 2020



Rules and Regulations for the Fashion's Landing - Walk of Fame:

1. Form a team of minimum 8 members to a maximum of 14 members.
2. Register as a team at our official website.
3. Make a payment of INR 2000 per team.
4. Remember to take a screenshot of the online transaction.
You need to produce a printed copy of the acknowledgement slip that you received on your successful transaction at the venue on the day of the event. Failing to which will lead to disqualification of the team.
5. The team members must be from the same college.
6. The team members should carry their college ID card on the day of the event. Students without the ID card will not be able to enter the venue.
7. There is no criteria for the theme of the show. You can prepare your own theme.
8. The judge's decision will be considered as final.
