About Utopia

UTOPIA is the annual culture fest of MCKV Institute Of Engineering. Every year the college holds this annual fest which is organized by the college students. Students from various colleges compete with other in the fields like drama, choreography, solo and group singing competition, fashion shows, war of bands, and various other fields. Students of the college perform skits, musical performances and plays. To make it more entertaining skilled performers from different genres are invited. Some major music bands and singers rock the students.

Where

MCKV Institute of Engineering, Liluah

When

Wednesday to Friday
29th to 31st January

FLIGHT OF FEET Inter-College Dance Competition

The inter-collegiate group dance competition is a platform for the best dancing troops to vaunt their charismatic moves. The competition invites all dance forms.

• Entry allowed on showing valid student ID cards only.
• This is an inter-collegiate dance event and there shall be only one team representing the college in UTOPIA 2020. (All participants of the group should represent one college only)
• Amount of registration fee (non-refundable & non-transferable) to be paid.
• Registrations will be closed if the limit exceeds, last date for the registration is 27th January,2020.
• Maximum external participants allowed: Solo-2, Duet-2, Group-3.
• Participants must get their songs/music in a pen-drive in .MP3 format only (as the other formats may not be supported). Live music is not allowed.
• Every participant should maintain the discipline, stick to the rules and participate in the true spirit of the competition.
• Usage of props is allowed. These are to be brought by the participant/team themselves. Organizers hold the discretion to allow the prop on stage.
• Any misconduct will lead to serious ramifications resulting in disqualification.
• Marks shall be deducted for exceeding the time limits.
• In case of malfunctioning of any equipment provided, the team will be allowed to repeat their performance from the beginning or from the point of disruption that the team wishes, with the consent of the judges.
• For Group Performances, number of groups should be at least two (2) otherwise there will be no prizes.
• There will be 1st prize for the winners of Duet only.
• Winner and Runners Up for Solo performance will be awarded.
